Hyper Island Brochure 2011/12
Overview

Hyper Island releases a brochure every year to show the work of its students, introduce its methodology and invite people to apply.

My Role

I was the Copy Editor for the 2011/2012 Hyper Island brochure. This involved reviewing grammar, spelling, punctuation and style. I clarified text, and wrote copy where it was needed.





Copywriting

I'm passionate about words. I've always found myself listening to the way words are used and the actual meaning they convey. I'm interested in the things that go unspoken, but are understood.

As part of my B.A. degree in Philosophy and History, I wrote my dissertation on Wittgenstein's account in the Philosophical Investigations of the relationship between words and ideas.

There's a huge difference in academic writing, and writing for an audience. I feel comfortable doing both. I like the challenge of stripping away excess words. I like the beauty of simple communication and I enjoy the experience of addressing an audience directly.
