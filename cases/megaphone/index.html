Running an Agency
Overview

To gain real-life experience of running an agency, one module at Hyper Island involved forming and running an agency. We named our agency Megaphone. I was the CEO.

My role

My management style is very adaptable. Certain groups have certain needs, and to impose a rigid management style can lead to uninvolved, unmotivated groups. I believe in discussing solutions rather than dictating terms. I believe in helping people realising their full potential, rather than setting unchallenging tasks. Above all, I believe in inclusion and transparency. At Megaphone, I listened to what our agency and our clients needed. From this, I was able to guide the agency in an informed and responsive way.

Projects

By the end of the module, we had designed the world's first Democracy app for Nacka Kommun (a municipality of Stockholm), as well as an app for Sweden's National Modern Art Museum. There was a competition run by Hyper Island to discover the best agency. We won.
