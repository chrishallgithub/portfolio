Exploring Technology
Overview

During Hyper Island's Exploring Technology module, my group developed a fun, but challenging memory game. We back-projected a flash-based game onto a custom built canvas, and detected the player's motion using a webcam. The above video (made by Younus) shows the game being played at the HI10 exhibition, an event organised to display my class's work.

My Role

I worked on concept development, research, testing and construction. I also built a prototype online scoring system using PHP and MySQL. For a while, we struggled to find a digital projector, and so I hacked one together out of an old monitor and an analogue projector.

Gamex Convention

We were later invited to display our game at the Gamex convention (the largest gaming convention in Scandinavia), which was a major honour.
