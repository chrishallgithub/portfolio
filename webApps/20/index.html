About
When working on a computer it is recommended to take a break every 20 minutes, and focus on something 20 feet away, for 20 seconds.

This app plays a sound and/or displays a pop-up alert to remind us that it is time to take a break.

Just leave this webpage open in a tab on your browser for it to work.
